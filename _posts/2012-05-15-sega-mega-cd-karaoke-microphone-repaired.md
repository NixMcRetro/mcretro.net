Good news, as you can see (and hear) in the above video the Sega Microphone has been repaired using a $10 part from [eBay](https://www.ebay.com.au/). A marvellous success! The particular type of microphone cartridge was a 600 ohm Impedance Dynamic Microphone Cartridge Black BG-58. I hope if anyone else out there needs this they will find it in the course of their journey.
