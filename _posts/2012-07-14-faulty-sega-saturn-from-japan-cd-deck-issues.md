You might remember the above video from a while back if you are a regular. The disc spins backward and the laser motor goes berserk as well. Enter chip marked BA6798S located on the left of the CD deck board. Rumour has it that this chip controls the motors. Since all my motors are going mad... It might just be worth a shot! So once more the white Saturn gets put onto a shelf and sidelined while we wait for parts.
