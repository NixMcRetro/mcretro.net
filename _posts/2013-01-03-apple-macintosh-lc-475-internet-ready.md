This is on the old Apple Macintosh LC 475 (aka Performa 475) running Mac OS 7.6.1. Works pretty well given the age of the thing, on par with a 486 DX2 66MHz. Even the versions of Netscape are pretty much the same as the 486 Windows versions.

I'd definitely recommend sticking to a more modern operating system if you are looking for a fully featured web browsing experience.
