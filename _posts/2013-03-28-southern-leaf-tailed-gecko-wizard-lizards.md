This is the second one of these leaf-tailed geckos that has come into my home over the years. Possibly the same one, they live for quite a while it turns out! Up to ten years in the wild.

They also don't appear to mind cooler temperatures, being active in the cold as low as -10°C. Hunting at night is a wise idea too to avoid predators. Smart gecko! :)
