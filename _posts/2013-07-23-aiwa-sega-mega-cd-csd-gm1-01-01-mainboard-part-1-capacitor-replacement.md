Electrolytic capacitors are notorious for wearing out and breaking down after 15-20 years. Here we have a great example, recapping an Aiwa Mega-CD boom box. Not the Sega Mega Drive part, just the Aiwa boom box part in this three part series of videos.

![](/assets/images/2013/img_0412a.jpg)

We pull out the mighty Hakko 808 desolder gun and replace the capacitors.

![](/assets/images/2013/img_0412b.jpg)

And for the most part, I think we can label this one as a partial win? I mean we got the CD drive LED to light up? On to the next part - Volume check!
