CUPCAKES! They worked! Never believe the hype! Makes 24 small or 12 large! :)

**Ingredients:** - 2 cups flour - 1 cup caster sugar - 2 eggs - 1/2 cup (125mL) of milk (maybe 3/4 cup) - 1 tsp of vanilla extract - 125g of butter - Pinch of salt

**Method:** Put everything in a bowl, then mix and put in cupcake patties. Then cook. Then eat. Victory will be yours, my friend!
