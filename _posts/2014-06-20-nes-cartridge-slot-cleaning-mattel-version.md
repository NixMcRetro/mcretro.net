We delve a little deeper into the NES and have a look at the cartridge slot. If you mess it up and it catches fire (for whatever reason) replacements can be found for about $10 shipped on eBay. Word of warning though, they are not as high quality as the original and the outer tin comes off a lot more readily than the original!
