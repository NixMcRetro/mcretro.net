Mayumi 4 supports three different modes on the SCPH-750x model / PU-22 board.  
To change modes, press and hold reset for two seconds.

Strongest Mode –> Old Modchip Mode –> Disable Mode

WDT\_OFF, ExtRC\_OSC, MCLR\_OFF
