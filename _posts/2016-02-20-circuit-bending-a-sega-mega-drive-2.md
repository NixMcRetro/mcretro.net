Here's a great little infographic on how to circuit bend your Mega Drive (Model 2) or Genesis (Model 2) courtesy of Gijs from [gieskes.nl](https://www.gieskes.nl/). You sure can get some exciting graphics, maybe I should do another video - if only it also broke the audio... actually to be honest I did mess around with the address / data lines on the audio chips on a Mega Drive (Model 1) but it froze every so often.

**Edit:** Looks like [gieskes.nl](https://www.gieskes.nl/) didn't read their datasheet for the VRAM... from [Console5](https://console5.com/techwiki/index.php?title=File:VRAM_512Kb_64K_x_8-bit_DIP_SOJ-pinout.jpg) we can see that there are two sets of VCC and VSS. Don't mess with them or something will fry.
