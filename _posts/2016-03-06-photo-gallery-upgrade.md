With [Google abandoning Picasa](http://googlephotos.blogspot.com/2016/02/moving-on-from-picasa.html) in the coming months I've decided to trial some of the other options out there. [Flickr](https://web.archive.org/web/20170810170045/https://www.flickr.com/photos/shanemcretro/albums) seems to be the best fit for the photo gallery moving forward but for the sake of scattering my photos everywhere I've also stuck them up on [Facebook](https://www.facebook.com/ShaneMcRetroPage). I'll leave them up on [Picasa](/goodies/) for as long as they stay there. Who knows it might even merge all over my [Google+](https://web.archive.org/web/20190309124724/https://plus.google.com/+ShaneMcRetro/posts/") page! :)

Update 2022-05-07: The Flickr account has been closed. The photo gallery will now live at [https://photos.mcretro.net](https://photos.mcretro.net).
