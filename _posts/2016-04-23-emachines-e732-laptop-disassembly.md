As I was pulling apart a laptop for a friend, I realised I should make it a video just so anyone else in the future who is doing the same can see what needs to be done. These are pretty easy to disassemble and are quite roomy if you have the base model without the advanced video chip (whatever that may have been). Plenty of room to grab the RAM, hard drive, and wifi card and swap them out with whatever you want. You could, possibly even swap the CPU if it is socketed. I didn't check that, but you can! :P
