Mission accomplished. Today on the 22nd June 2016 I have finally reached 88.0kg. This puts me at the lowest weight in my recorded history. Records date back to October 2008, a mere eight years ago. Alright, maybe not that mere. Chicken and milk. That's how it was _mostly_ done. Let have a look at some more cool screen caps though. First up, the last six months (or thereabouts) of lean / fat percentages. I like where the trends are heading.

To think I was happy to go from "Obese" to "Overweight", imagine what it will be like when I hit "Healthy"! Might be a good day for... cake? Or was that all a lie...
