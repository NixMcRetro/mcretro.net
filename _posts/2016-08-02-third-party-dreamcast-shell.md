I've bumped a smoke/sun tainted Dreamcast into a fresh new smokey translucent case. Fits in pretty well with the This is COOL Saturn. I had a Broadband Adapter (BBA) in this particular Dreamcast so I dremelled away at the modem port to make the RJ11 socket an RJ45 size.

For once... it worked. Usually when it comes to making things look tidy I end up melting it or at least some sort of firestorm. Not today, not today. ;)
