That's it, I'm sick of mobile phones and always being switched on in the digital world. Modern phones are such a joke with their 60-minute-man-like batteries.

{% include youtube.html id="NlZqfNNtvVM" %}

All I use my phone for is SMS and that's usually just 2FA rubbish. Don't get me wrong, I love getting two almost two days out of my phone but it's time to move backwards.

Enter the Nokia 301.

![Nokia 301 Blue](/assets/images/2016/img_0538.jpg)

The battery apparently lasts over a month in standby, which is all my phone does. One month or MORE! Thank you candybar phones! Maybe when the next batch of iPhones roll out and there's a 4" overpowered phone available for a reasonable price I'll switch back. At any rate, it's time to sell my old iPhone 5s and move on.
