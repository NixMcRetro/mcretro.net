Lately I've noticed folk have been replacing their TeraDrive power supplies either due to failure or needing 220v power in place of a big transformer sitting on their desk, I'm the latter. In the following photos we have the TeraDrive being retrofitted with a [Mean Well PT-65B](https://www.ebay.com.au/) open frame PSU.

![photo1](/assets/images/2016/img_0566.jpg)

It's actually quite a good fit inside the existing power supply as you can see. The PT-65B also has a negative rail if you wanted to wire up your ISA slots - TeraDrives don't have any negative rails. Anyway, enjoy the photos - courtesy of DeChief on Assembler Games.
