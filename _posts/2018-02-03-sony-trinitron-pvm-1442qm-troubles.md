Here's a quick overview of the Sony Trinitron PVM 1442QM I picked up a while back on eBay, from the UK of all places. It made it across mostly intact, a little bit of frame damage here and there but otherwise functional.

![](/assets/images/2018/img_0614.jpg)

![](/assets/images/2018/img_0615.jpg)

At the very least it has a tonne of inputs and outputs! It's a decent size too, unlike the 9044QM I have sitting around somewhere.
