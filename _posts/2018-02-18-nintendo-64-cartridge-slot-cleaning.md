Cartridge slot cleaning is one of my favourite adventures to embark on. This applies to many other consoles that are cartridge based. Just be sure to not get any extra fluff off your paper towel into the cartridge slot, that's a big no-no!

Don't forget to clean your cartridges with a plastic eraser as well! :D
