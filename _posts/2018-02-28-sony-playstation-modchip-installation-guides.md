PIC microchips 12C508, 12C508A, 12C509, 12C509A, 12F508, and 12F629 are all cross-compatible as they have the same pinout as each other.

**Mayumi 4** - [SCPH-7502 / PU-22](/mayumi-4-pic12c508a-scph-7502-pu-22/)

**MultiMode3 (MM3)** - SCPH-750X

Am I missing any models? Send me a message via the [contact](/about/) page and I'll put a guide together for the particular model and chip you have! :)
