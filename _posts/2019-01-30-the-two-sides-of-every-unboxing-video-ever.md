The first side is as vanilla and ordinary as you would imagine an unboxing video to be. The controller in the box is extracted through the process one would usually expect. You could consider this the safe for work (SFW) or safe for life (SFL) version.

{% include youtube.html id="njfIGVedRWg" %}

The second side of our coin reveals a much darker not safe for work (NSFW) or not safe for life (NSFL) version. I can only imagine after creating unboxing video after unboxing video, the mind of a serial unboxer becomes somewhat demented. Their sweetest dreams are filled with torn boxes, ripped packaging and discarded manuals.

I needed the controllers without boxes, so the boxes were laid to rest. Rest in peace box #1. Rest in many pieces box #2.
