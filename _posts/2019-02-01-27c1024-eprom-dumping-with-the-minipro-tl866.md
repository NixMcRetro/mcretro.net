Ah yes, the ways of the 16-bit chip. Here we dump a Mega-CD BIOS several times to ensure we aren't getting any errors. It's also a good idea to reseat the chip in between dumps to make sure you are getting the best out of your 27C1024. Mind you, I was quite happy to see the TL866CS support the 27C1024.
