See that little purple ".onion available" note? Thanks to the work by the Tor Project [here](https://community.torproject.org/onion-services/advanced/onion-location/) they've got a way to present clearnet (normal) internet sites to route through tor at the click of a button. Of course this has to be through the Tor Browser, which has made leaps and bounds in recent times.

![](/assets/images/2020/img_0680.jpg)

And... onion activated! One click (or preference) and you can be defaulting to Tor sites instead of their clearnet equivalent, pretty neat! No more remembering chirpys54 soda something as the address. I should move all the remaining sites from v2 domains to v3 domains at this stage. It involves editing the header and I'm not sure it supports multiple onion sites. v3 onion domains have their own advantages too of course! ?
