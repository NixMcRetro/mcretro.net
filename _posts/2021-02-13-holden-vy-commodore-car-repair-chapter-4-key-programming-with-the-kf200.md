These car keys with batteries in them are ticking time bombs. Batteries do not last forever and you can typically expect 10 years on these Commodores. This one was well overdue. Enter the KF200. I like to think of it as the King Flasher 200. It was kind enough to let me reprogram a new key head. I think the key head was AU$47.30! Isn't technology great? Picked it up from Autobarn somewhere north of Sydney a few years back. Here's the video that documents that journey!
