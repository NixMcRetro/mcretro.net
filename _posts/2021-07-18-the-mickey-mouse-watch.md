Ah yes, the Mickey Mouse watch from my childhood. It broke down a long time ago and never got repaired. As it turns out, these watches are very complicated internally - I never stood a chance! Being unable to repair the existing mechanism, I did the next best thing, an internal swap maintaining the same face and outer casing.

And, it turned out quite well, a little annoying that I had to have so many smaller versions turn up before I found one of a suitable size. Thankfully it is all over now with nothing more to do. It's packed away safely for whenever I need to call on Batman. Then again, this wasn't the Batman watch, was it?
