I have nothing but praise for the Sakura Wars franchise. I may have only played the original Sakura Wars (1996) on my Sega Saturn thanks to the [translation patch](https://www.romhacking.net/translations/5318/).

The remake was done very well. Battle scenes felt much faster and the story stayed pretty much the same, telling a story of secret ninja warriors living their lives in [Japan's Edo period](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Edo_period). At least I think it was ninjas. Might be time for me to rewatch the playthrough.

On that topic, the playlist at the top of this post includes all the live streams. I hope you enjoy watching me power through the story in my mechanised spirit armour.
