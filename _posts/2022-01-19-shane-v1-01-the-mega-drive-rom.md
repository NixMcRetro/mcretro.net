shanE v1.01 was compiled for the Sega Mega Drive sometime in February 2016. I was supposed to write something about this over five years ago but it apparently got away from me.

![](/assets/images/2022/img_0796.jpg)

shanE v1.00 (above) is pretty simple, I edited the sample source code and compiled the ROM under Ubuntu if I remember correctly. Here is [the source and my compiled ROMs](/assets/uploads/shanE_SRC.zip). shanE v1.01 gets a little more complicated.

I can't quite remember what I compiled it with though. Wait! Ah yes, it must have been [SGDK](https://github.com/Stephane-D/SGDK) by Stephane Dallongeville. [One of the samples](https://github.com/Stephane-D/SGDK/blob/master/sample/basics/hello-world/src/main.c) looks very familiar!

They also have a [Patreon](https://www.patreon.com/SGDK) if you want to support them. I'm glad to see the project is still being worked on after all this time. Maybe I'll revisit it sometime in the future, regardless - Thanks Stephane!
