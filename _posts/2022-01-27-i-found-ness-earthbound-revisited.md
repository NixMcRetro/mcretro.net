Some further digging into my "to be sorted" folder has revealed some save games for EarthBound. They appear to date back to October 2013. Talk about a blast from the past!

![](/assets/images/2022/img_0808.jpg)

I had two saves saved at level 75 and level 97. The amount of grinding involved between those two would have been massive. Well worth it though.

![](/assets/images/2022/img_0807.jpg)

It looks like I had been using [SnemulDS](https://web.archive.org/web/20190516030442/http://www.snemul.com/ds/?page_id=2), which was [last updated in 2007](https://www.zophar.net/consoles/nds/snes/snemulds.html). The official site appears to be down as of 2022 and looks like it might have been killed off sometime in 2019.

![](/assets/images/2022/img_0810.jpg)

EarthBound still rates among the best games I have ever played for the Super Nintendo. But I was playing it on a DS Lite so I could take it anywhere I wanted, if I wanted to play in bed or on the couch - done!

With my original DS Lite from Japan long since sold, today I'm on a Mac and was able to fire it up in [OpenEmu](https://github.com/OpenEmu/OpenEmu) under [SNES9x 1.6](http://www.snes9x.com/). SNES9x really does go [wayback](https://web.archive.org/web/19981202225201/http://www.snes9x.com/)!

![](/assets/images/2022/img_0809.jpg)

So for now we must once again say goodbye to Ness and his friends, smiles and tears, smiles and tears...
