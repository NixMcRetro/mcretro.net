The AM/FM switch felt stuck down and spongy - on AM. Maybe FM wasn't used much at all on this radio. The dust collected and got in over the years. The great news is that a tiny bit of CRC 2-26 and it's loosened up quite well. That stuff really works wonders on gummed up switches. Listen to that new click! ✅
