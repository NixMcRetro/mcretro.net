Firstly, I would like to give a hats off to the developers. This game did not crash once on my PlayStation 4 **_slim_** and performance was on par with Horizon Zero Dawn.

![](/assets/images/2022/img_0990.jpg)

Plus, I only got stuck in a wall once! And after Cyberpunk 2077... I'd get happily get stuck in the wall ten times before going back to that dumpster fire. This is how a game should be made, take note Cyberpunk! As for the story itself, it is incredible!

On the game play front, it feels like a combination of Fallout 4 (post-apocalyptic), Cyberpunk (blue/pink holograms) and Zelda/Metroid (puzzle solving) all in an open world. Oh look, it's the end of 2022! :-)
