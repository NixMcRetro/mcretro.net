Recently I had the joy of being able to replace the capacitors on a Miss Melody ES688. The poor thing had NCK branded caps, which are way past their best before date. Installed some Panasonics and Rubycons and it's ready to roll. Just need a machine to test it in.

![](/assets/images/2023/img_0999.jpg)

Those old capacitors were way past their prime and couldn't even be found on the [big list](/capacitors-manufacturer-tier-list-ranking/) of decent to lesser decent capacitors.

![](/assets/images/2023/img_1000.jpg)

More information on this card can be found [here](https://amoretro.de/2011/06/miss-melody-ess-audiodrive-688-es688.html) and [here](https://vccollection.ru/?page_id=4860). A good place to start looking for the drivers, if needed, is also [here](https://www.vogons.org/viewtopic.php?f=62&t=70102&start=20).
