This elegant snake-eyed skink (Cryptoblepharus pulcher) skink was delivered to me on a winter evening. It was found in a warehouse trapped under [old computer equipment](http://forum.acms.org.au). It was likely trapped there for a long time, in the cold with no food or water. It looked extremely malnourished and as a result was very lethargic.

![](/assets/images/2023/img_1125.jpg)

I would love to say everything turned out great, but I can't. The natural world knows no mercy. There is a strong possibility that the lizard could no longer eat and has since either starved or has been killed by one of the stronger lizards. There are no guarantees in life - we can only do our best. 🦎
