Flicky! Ohhh that little cicada was such a survivor. Resting and laying eggs for almost every day for ten days while in captivity. Sadly she succumbed to time on 8th March 2023.

All these events all happened just about one year ago. You can view the videos below or on either my [Super Nature World playlist](https://www.youtube.com/playlist?list=PLbei9YJqem2nKHPaowFMeLX_8osOJS_v7) or [Shane McRetro playlist](https://www.youtube.com/playlist?list=PLJAZP-TLMPj5agB1oCwFcS8rs0BpDd8Pb). It doesn't all end here though as Flicky's children eventually hatch. 🪲
